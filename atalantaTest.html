Goe to the woman washing cloaths, and doe you after the same manner.

and the woman dye, and the toad be grosse with milke
Sow your gold in the white foliated earth

Take an egge and smite it with a fiery sword

eating of the fruit thereof hee will become young
Give fire to fire, Mercury to Mercury, and you have enough

of his Sonne Jupiter, is placed in Helicon as a monument to men.

times washd in a river, as Naaman the leper in Jordan

instruct you

If you kill one of the four they will all suddenly dye
Nature teacheth nature how to subdue fire

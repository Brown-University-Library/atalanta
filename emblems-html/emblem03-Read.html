Emblem 3. Of the Secrets of Nature. Go to the woman washing clothes, and do you after the same manner.

Epigram 3.


If you delight to dive in things abstruse,
Be vigilant, observe what may conduce:
Do you not see, how women wash and boil
Their clothes in waters hot, if they get soil?
Pursue their steps, you cannot go astray,
For water washeth any filth away.

English Translation of Atalanta Fugiens

The pages below are transcriptions of the English translation of the Atalanta Fugiens from Yale Beinecke Mellon MS 48. These files contain both the original and regularized text - you will notice that there are highlighted sections that contain the same word twice, once in archaic spelling and again, modernized. If you are citing a passage, you only need to include the normalized spelling. Erasures, underlining and other editorial interventions are also marked.
